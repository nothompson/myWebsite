Slime Mold Granulator Winter 25

Final project for algorithmic composition course. Visual algorithm written with processing, with OSC connection to a Max patch. Granulating source material with parameters linked to slime mold agents.

Prototype Pitch Shifter VST Winter 25

Final project for digital audio programming course. Written in C++ with the JUCE framework, and incorporates claymation assets for the rotary knobs and background.

Fleshbox Fall 24

Final project for digital audio programming course. Algorithmic music box with granular controls, written entirely in Max MSP's gen~ environment. Exported to daisy seed with oopsy~. Hardened clay shell fitted to protoboard. Thank you Karl!

Music Video Commission Summer 24

Sample Pack Promotion and Collaboration Summer 24

Claymation promotion and sound contribution for Imago Meri, a production tools group. Includes 80 melodic oneshots for producers to use in their sound crafting. I used Xfer Serum and supercollider to synthesize the source material for my contributions.

Performance of Generative Max Patch at MAT House Show Fall 24, made Spring 24

Claymation and chaotic attractor simulation in Jitter. Mouse can navigate mixing interface and swap between algorithimic or controlled distortion lead. Particle equations, granular controls, feedback knob, and lead notes all controlled by xbox controller. Aiming to make Max patches interactive like apart of a music video game or alien daw. Shredding on a controller!

Supercollider with Webcam controls via TouchDesigner Spring 24

Final project for Curtis Road's Music and Technology class. Pre-composed structure via supercollider tasks, with chaotic parameters that ensure a unique listening experience on every iteration. Everything synthesized from scratch. MediaPipe is a google Machine Learning framework that allows my webcam to capture where my fingers are and send OSC data to live granular controls in the supercollider code. All granular glyphs are made with claymation except the bottom left, which is stop-motion of me cracking a stone. It was an absolute honor being able to take a course under Curtis Roads and I am so grateful for the experience.

Bird Sound Design Study Spring 24

Final sound design project for Birds, sonic arts and environmental sensitivities course. Everything is fully synthesized in Xfer Serum, aside from some textures derived from field recordings. Visual includes a hand-animated flying cycle, colored pencil on paper.

Score Work for "leafmold" Spring 24
